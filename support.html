Support

Welcome to Lean Neutrino support. Find answers to common questions below, or reach out directly if you need help with something not covered here.

Features

  • Workouts — Five structured dumbbell and gym strength workouts (Back & Legs, Chest & Tricep, Bicep & Back, Glute & Leg, Shoulders) plus Cardio (Run and Bicycle). The app follows a pre-set weekly schedule with Tuesday and Wednesday as rest days.
  • Consistency Score — A score from 0 to 1 calculated from your daily, weekly, and monthly workout history over a rolling 21-day window.
  • Step Tracker — Reads your step count from Apple Health, with a 10,000-step daily goal.
  • Weight Log — Log your bodyweight in kg and see how it has changed over the last 30 days.
  • Daily Challenge — An AI-generated mini challenge each day, personalised to your scheduled workout and current streak. Tap the refresh icon to get a new one.
  • Streak & Weekly View — See which days this week you completed strength or cardio, and track your day streak.
  • Themes — Choose from multiple dark and light themes in Settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my step count showing?

Lean Neutrino reads step data from Apple Health. Make sure you have granted the app permission to read steps:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone.
  • Go to Privacy & Security → Health → Lean Neutrino.
  • Enable Steps under "Read Data".

If steps still don't appear, try closing and reopening the app.

Can I change the workout schedule?

The current version uses a fixed weekly plan. Custom scheduling is not available yet but may be added in a future update.

How is the Consistency Score calculated?

The score is a number between 0 and 1 that reflects how consistently you have been working out across the last 21 days, taking into account daily, weekly, and monthly patterns. A score of 1.00 means perfect consistency over that window.

How do I delete my data?

You can erase all your workout sessions, weight entries, and daily challenges from within the app:

  • Tap the Settings icon (top-right of the home screen).
  • Scroll to the Data section.
  • Tap Reset All Data and confirm.

To delete your account and all associated data from our servers, email me at the address below and I'll take care of it within 7 days.

How do I sign out?

Tap the Settings icon on the home screen, then tap Sign Out under the Account section.

How do I change my name?

Go to Settings → Profile, type your first name, and tap Save. Your name appears in the greeting on the home screen.

The Daily Challenge isn't loading — what should I do?

The daily challenge is generated by an AI model and requires an internet connection. Check that your device is online and reopen the app. You can also tap the refresh icon on the challenge card to regenerate it.
