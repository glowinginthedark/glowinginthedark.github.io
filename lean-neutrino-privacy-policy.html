Privacy Policy

Lean Neutrino  ·  Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bilal Zubair ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use Lean Neutrino (the "App").

1. Information I Collect

The App collects the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided via Sign in with Apple.
  • Fitness and health data — step count data read from Apple HealthKit on your device.
  • Workout data — workout sessions you log, including exercise type, duration, and self-reported effort.
  • Weight data — body weight entries you manually log in the App.
  • Usage data — daily challenge completions and consistency scores generated from your activity.

2. How I Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the App's features:

  • Authenticating your account and syncing your data across sessions.
  • Displaying your fitness progress, streaks, and consistency score.
  • Generating personalised daily challenges and coaching feedback using an AI model.
  • Tracking your step count alongside your workouts.

I do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the App's core functionality.

3. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — used for authentication and cloud storage of your workout, weight, and challenge data. Data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API — used to generate personalised daily challenges and coaching blurbs. Workout context (e.g. workout type and streak length) is sent to Google's API to produce these responses. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple HealthKit — step count data is read from your device's HealthKit store. This data is not shared with any third party beyond what is described above.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account and fitness data is stored securely on Supabase's servers. Health data (step counts) is read directly from your device and is not uploaded to any server. I take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as you have an active account. You can delete all your stored data at any time from within the App via Settings → Reset All Data. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact me at the email address below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact me so I can delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
